        Preparation and Safety

        Cooking instructions: other

        To panfry / oven cook from chilled. If freezing at home, defrost thoroughly before cooking. Before Cooking: Pre heat oven to 180C Fan / 200C/ Gas Mark 6. Preheat frying pan to a medium high heat. Remove all packaging. During Cooking: Reduce pan to medium heat. Pan fry burgers in a dry frying pan for 3 minutes on each side. Remove burgers from the pan, place onto an oven tray, loosely cover with foil and cook in the centre of the oven for 18 minutes. After Cooking: Remove from the oven and serve. Check food is piping hot throughout, all juices run clear and no pink colour remains. All cooking appliances vary. This is a guide only. To pan fry / oven cook from chilled: Medium heat 24 minutes

        Warnings:

        This product contains raw meat.

        This product is not suitable for rare cooking. Do not undercook burgers. Keep raw meat separate from cooked foods. Use separate kitchen utensils and surfaces for raw meat and cooked foods or wash them thoroughly between use. Wash hands after handling raw meat. Raw meat may contain organisms hazardous to health. Cook thoroughly until piping hot throughout, no pink colour remains and the juices run clear. If not fully cooked in accordance with the instructions harmful bacteria may remain.
